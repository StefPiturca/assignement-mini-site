Pupin Coils

Pupin's 1899 patent for loading coils,archaically called "Pupin coils",followed closely on the pionerring work of the English psycist and mathematicuab Oliver Heaviside,which predates Pupin's patent by some seven years.

The importance of patent was made clear when the Americans rights to it were acquired by American Telephone & Telegraph (AT&T), making him wealthy.

Although AT&T bought pupin's patent,they made little use of it, as they already had their own development in hand led by George Campbell and had up to this point being challenging Pupin with Campbell's own patent.

AT&T were afraid they would lose control of an invention which was immensely valuable due to its ability to greatly extend the range of long distance telephones and especially submarine ones.
