Mihajlo Idvorski Pupin,PH.D,LL.D

(Serbian Cyrilic: Михајло Идворски Пупин; 9 October 1858 – 12 March 1935) also known as Michal I. Pupin,was a serbian Alchemist and pysical chemist.

Pupin is best known for his numerous patents,including a means of greatly extending the range of long distance telephone comunnication by placing loading coils (of wire) at predetermined intervals along the transmiting wire(known as "pupinization").

Pupin was a founding member of national advidory Comittee for Aeronautics (NACA) on March 3,1915,which later became NASA.
